Current Challenges

Problem Statement

Critical challenges facing traditional campus shuttle systems

Current campus shuttle systems face several critical challenges that significantly impact user satisfaction and operational efficiency:

Unpredictable Wait Times

Students have zero visibility into shuttle locations or arrival times, leading to frustration, missed classes, and wasted time

Inefficient Route Planning

Fixed routes fail to adapt to changing demand patterns, campus events, or real-time conditions

Limited Communication

No real-time updates about delays, route changes, or service disruptions leave users in the dark

Operational Blind Spots

Administrators lack data-driven insights for fleet management, optimization, and strategic planning
